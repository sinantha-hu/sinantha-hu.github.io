ABOUT

I am a population health researcher based in Vientiane, Lao PDR. I manage a large multi-generational birth cohort in two central and two periphery districts of Vientiane Capital under the supervision of Dr. Günther Fink with Swiss Tropical Public Health Institute and Dr. Somphou Sayasone at the Lao Tropical and Public Health Institute. My research interests include neglected tropical disease transmission and interconnected human and planetary health.
